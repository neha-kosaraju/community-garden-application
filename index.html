Community Garden - Application Form

If you are interested in renting a plot at our community garden, please submit this application by the end of January. Each plot has an area of 64 sq ft.(8ft. by 8ft.), and the maximum number of plots that can be rented is 6. There are limited plots available, so those who have early submissions will have higher chances of securing a plot. We will review applications and get back to you in February, just in time for you to prepare your garden for spring!
